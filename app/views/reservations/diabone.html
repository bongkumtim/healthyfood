Nasi Lemak & Cornflake Chicken

INFORMATIONS ABOUT YOUR DISH :

45 mins
calories: 333kcal. fat: 10g. carbohydrates: 36g. protein: 24g.
by Chef Ravindran a/l Mathavan (Columbia Asia Hospital-Seremban)
Rice
1. 175g uncooked brown rice
2. 150 ml low fat milk
3. 3 pandan leaves (blended)
4. pinch of salt (to taste)
5. 70ml trimmed coconut milk (optional)

Crispy Cornflake Chicken
1. 350g chicken (sliced)
2. 1 medium egg (for coating)
3. 5g cornflakes (for coating)
4. 20g multi-purpose flour (for coating)
5. salt and pepper, to taste
6. coarsely ground black pepper, to taste

Sambal
1. 20g dried chillies
2. 100g shallots
3. 1 tbsp vegetable oil
4. Salt, to taste (pinch)

Fried Kangkung
1. 200g kangkung (water spinach)
2. 1 small onion, sliced
3. 1 red chilli, sliced
4. 1 tbsp vegetable oil

Acar Rampai
1. 1 tbsp vegetable oil
2. 75g cucumber, cut into strips
3. 75g carrot, cut into strips
4. 2 shallots
5. 2 cloves garlic
6. 2 whole star anise
7. 2 cinnamon sticks
8. 2 tsp curry powder
9. 1 tbsp chilli sauce
10.1 tsp vinegar
11.salt and pepper, to taste
Direction
1. Mix low fat milk and blended pandan leaf with washed brown rice. Cook with a rice cooker.
2. Coat the chicken sliced with multi-purpose flour, eggs and lastly cornflake. Bake in oven for 8 minutes at 160°C.
3. Heat oil in non-stick pan. Sauté onion till fragrance. Add vegetable.
4. Heat oil in non-stick pan. Stir-fried all ingredients together.
5. Sauté blended shallot and dry chili until fragrance.
